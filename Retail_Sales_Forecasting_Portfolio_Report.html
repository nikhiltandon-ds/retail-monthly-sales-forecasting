1. Business Framework & Objective

The Core Challenge: Supply chains often face inventory imbalances, causing stockouts of high-demand items during seasonal spikes and excessive holding costs for slow-moving categories.

The Solution: Built a multi-model forecasting framework to predict future monthly store revenue. This pipeline helps retail procurement managers optimize warehouse space allocation, align purchase triggers, and maintain capital liquidity.

2. Mathematical Engineering & Pipeline Mechanics

The time-based data ingestion script executes key verification steps before fitting the predictive model models:

  • Temporal Resampling: Aggregated granular transactional logs into structured equidistant monthly datetime indices using pandas .resample('MS').sum().
  • Stationarity Validation: Evaluated moving sequence variances via the Augmented Dickey-Fuller (ADF) check. Applied first-order differentiation adjustments to stabilize non-stationary upward trend vectors.
  • Seasonality Decomposition: Isolated the baseline dataset into clear underlying components: the structural overall growth trajectory, cyclical seasonal swings, and random residual noise.
